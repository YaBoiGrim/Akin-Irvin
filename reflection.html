What do you think trust means?

I think trust means to believe in people who you can rely on when something important happens. I trust the people I keep close to me like my Mom and Dad, my cousin, my older siblings, and a few of my close friends. I think you should only trust the people you can rely on for anything because if you can’t, they may end up being the wrong person to trust.
